Your website is often the first interaction a prospective patient has with your practice. Done well, it builds trust within seconds. Done poorly, it sends them straight to a competitor.

## 1. Mobile-first design

More than 70% of healthcare searches in South Africa happen on mobile. Your site must load fast, look great, and let patients book on a small screen.

## 2. Online booking

A simple "Book an appointment" button — connected to a real calendar — converts far better than a contact form.

## 3. Clear service pages

Each service should have its own page that explains what it is, what to expect, and what it costs (or how to find out).

## 4. Authentic photography

Stock photos signal "generic". Real photos of your team and rooms signal "trustworthy".

## 5. POPIA-compliant forms

Any form collecting patient information needs a clear privacy notice and consent checkbox.

## 6. Reviews and testimonials

Social proof from real patients (with permission) drives bookings — particularly for new patients.

## 7. Strong local SEO

Your Google Business Profile should be linked, your address consistent everywhere, and your service areas clearly stated.
