When a patient searches "dentist near me" or "GP in Rondebosch", the top three local results capture most of the bookings. Here's how to be one of them.

## Optimise your Google Business Profile

Complete every field. Add photos monthly. List every service you offer. Use your real practice name — no keyword stuffing.

## Earn reviews — consistently

Aim for one or two new reviews per week. Reply to every single one (positive and negative) within 48 hours.

## Build local citations

List your practice on Healthbridge, RecoMed, Yellow Pages, and your medical association directory. Keep your name, address, and phone identical everywhere.

## Optimise your service pages

Each page should target a specific service plus a location ("Botox in Sandton", "Paediatrician in Pretoria East"). Include FAQ sections that match what patients actually ask.

## Track what matters

Map pack impressions, direction requests, and phone calls from your Google Business Profile are leading indicators of new patient enquiries.
