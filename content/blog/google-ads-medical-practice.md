Google Ads can fill your appointment book — or burn through your budget in a week. The difference comes down to setup, targeting, and tracking.

## Start with intent, not volume

A search for "best dermatologist Sandton" is worth ten times more than "skin care tips". Build campaigns around the keywords that signal a patient ready to book.

## Geo-target tightly

Most practices serve patients within a 15–20 km radius. Don't pay for clicks from across the country.

## Use negative keywords

Block searches like "free", "DIY", "home remedy" — they drain budget without producing bookings.

## Track real conversions

A click is not a patient. Use call tracking and form submissions as your conversion goals so you can calculate cost-per-patient.

## Stay HPCSA-compliant

Avoid superlatives, guarantees, and patient testimonials in ad copy. Stick to factual, helpful descriptions of your service.
